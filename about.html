Introduction (click to toggle)

I was born and raised in New York, and have found my way to Nashville over the years. I am a lifelong artist, a shark lover, a sports fanatic, and enjoyer of any creative expression.
Having a curiosity about the human condition and emotion led me to filmmaking and acting, which has taught me invaluable lessons in the connection between human experience and visual communication.
Because of this, I got into design a few years ago and have fallen in love with the processes, concepts, and theories of UX/UI between attending boot camps and reading and practicing concepts in my free time.
When I am not designing, I may be reading, making short films, acting, training at the gym, or hanging out with my dog, Ozzy.
